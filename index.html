⚡ About CHOP'D

La Mirada's Fresh Food
Destination Since Day One.

CHOP'D was born from a simple idea: healthy food should taste incredible, fill you up, and not cost a fortune. We got tired of overpriced salads with skimpy portions and bland flavors, so we built the salad shop we always wished existed.

Every salad is chopped fresh when you order. Every bowl is built with care. Every smoothie is blended with real fruit. We believe that eating well should never feel like a compromise, and one bite of our food proves it.
